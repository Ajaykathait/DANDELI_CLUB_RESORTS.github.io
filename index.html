Dandeli is a town in the western Indian state of Karnataka that is located on the banks of River Kali and surrounded by mesmerizing green lush and dense woodland.

The place is encompassed with many popular places like-

Dandeli Wildlife Sanctuary is an abode to animals like monkeys, elephants including black panthers along with various bird species and also abundant plant diversities.
Kavala Caves is a notable temple of limestone caves that could offer you a lifetime remembering the adventurous experience as the temple is completely dark inside including snakes and bats.
Anshi National Park which got renamed as Kali Tiger Reserve is a complete package to withstand nature comprising deep valleys, flowing rivers with scenic mountains having a good balance of flora and fauna.
